Frequently Asked Questions

Device Scheme

What is the MTN TD Sterling Device Financing Scheme?The Device Financing Scheme is an opportunity afforded to eligible customers to drive the adoption and uptake of smart devices through the provision of affordable credit facilities within specified timelines with monthly instalment payments.
‍
Who is eligible to apply?All Customers that meet our specified criteria.
How do I register?You can register by visiting any of the approved MTN experience centres to provide the required details for registration.
‍
What is the Maximum Loan Tenure for the scheme?6 months.
What is the required length of salary account relationship?The scheme is open to all Customers and not only Salary earners once you can provide access to 3 months Bank statements for your most active accounts from any Bank.
